# Privacy Policy – AppReel

**Last updated: March 2026**

This privacy policy describes how the AppReel application ("the app", "we") collects, uses, and protects information in the context of mobile app usage.

## 1. Data Controller

Data is processed in the context of using the AppReel application. For any questions regarding this policy or your data, you may contact us using the contact details provided on the app's store listing.

## 2. Data Collected and Purposes

### 2.1 App Usage Data

- **Local data**: the app may locally store on your device information related to your usage (e.g. browsing history, preferences) in order to improve your experience. This data remains on your device unless you reset the app or device data.

- **Technical data**: technical information (device type, operating system, language) may be used to ensure the proper functioning and compatibility of the app.

### 2.2 Advertising

The app displays advertisements provided by third-party partners. Depending on the version or configuration of the app, one or more of the following services may be used. These partners may collect and process data to serve ads, measure their effectiveness, and personalize advertising content.

#### AppLovin (MAX)

The app may use the AppLovin MAX SDK to display advertisements (including rewarded ads). AppLovin and its partners may collect or receive data such as:

- Device advertising identifier (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- IP address
- App usage data
- Technical data (device model, operating system, language)

**Purposes**: ad delivery and personalization, advertising performance measurement, fraud prevention.

- AppLovin Privacy Policy: [https://www.applovin.com/privacy/](https://www.applovin.com/privacy/)

#### Google (AdMob)

The app may use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. Google may collect or receive data such as:

- Device advertising identifier (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- IP address
- App usage data
- Technical data

**Purposes**: ad delivery and personalization, performance measurement, analytics.

- Google Privacy Policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- AdMob and Privacy Information: [https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## 3. Legal Basis and Consent

- For personalized advertising, processing may be based on your consent (e.g. via tracking settings on your device or choices offered within the app).
- You may withdraw your consent or limit ad targeting at any time through your device settings (e.g. "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS, advertising settings on Android).

## 4. Data Retention

- Data stored locally on your device is retained for as long as the app is installed (or until reset).
- Data processed by advertising partners is subject to their own retention policies (see links above).

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have rights such as: access to your data, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection, and portability. For data processed by third parties (AppLovin, Google), you may exercise your rights directly with those partners or through your device settings.

## 6. Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect data processed within the app. Third-party partners are required to comply with their own security and privacy obligations.

## 7. Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of the last update is indicated at the top of this document. An up-to-date version will be accessible within the app and, where applicable, via the URL provided on the app's store listing.

## 8. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your personal data, please use the contact details provided on the app's store listing (App Store / Play Store) or within the app.
